  • Usage Guide

    1. In the search bar at the top of the page, type in the name of the city you want to see the weather for and click the "Search" button.

    2. The temperature is displayed in Celsius by default.

    3. The humidity is displayed as a percentage, and the wind speed is displayed in kilometers per hour.

    4. The cloud percentage shows how much of the sky is covered by clouds.

    5. The sunrise time is displayed in 24-hour format.

    6. To view your favorite cities, click on the "Favorites" tab in the navigation bar.
